For International Buyers

What matters most to a UHNWI acquiring in Monaco

Buying

No restriction on foreign ownership. No annual property tax. Full transaction handled by a Monegasque notary — expect 6–9% in acquisition costs. Residency is a separate, distinct process from ownership and can be pursued in parallel.

Renting

Long-term leases typically require 3 months' deposit and proof of funds. Furnished residences in Larvotto and Monte-Carlo command a premium and turn over quickly — decisions are made in days, not weeks.
